Design Principles

PARC: Repetition

Lauren Conrad

GabyVer website

This is the website for Lauren Conra, in this website we can see hoe the principle of repetition is used on the whole website. The principle of repetition simply means the reusing of the same or similar elements throughout your design. Repetition of certain design elements in a design will bring a clear sense of unity, consistency, and cohesiveness.

PARC: Proximity

Deseret Book

Deseret Book website

This is the website for Deseret Book, this website shows how to apply proximity when having a lot of content. We can use proximity to create relationships between elements to aid in understanding the information presented. A good sense of proximity in design can help differentiate visual elements to reduce visual clutter and make design more comprehensible. Proximity is influential to the balance and hierarchy design principles. Space between visual elements will communicate a particular dynamic on a page.

White Space and Clean Design

Apple

Apple website

This is the website for Apple where we can notice a very clean design. White space or negative space is simply unmarked space in the design. It is the space between the layouts, lines of paragraphs, between paragraphs, between different UI elements and so on. White space does not literally mean an empty space with a white background.
